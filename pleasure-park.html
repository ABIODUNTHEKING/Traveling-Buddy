Lekki Conservation Center

In the oil-rich garden city of Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria, there is a public leisure area called Port Harcourt Pleasure Park. It is strategically situated on a large open area with round-the-clock internal security along the main Aba Road, bordered by the Army Barracks (also known as Bori Camp) and Air Force base junction.

The park is run by an international team from Julius Berger Nigeria Plc and serves as both a tourist attraction and a source of income for the government. The park has a variety of amenities for people of all ages, including a 5-star movie theater and an international restaurant that was opened to go with the park's amenities. The Park's natural and tranquil setting will provide you the chance to relax because all amenities are kept immaculately clean.

Oniru Private Beach

The Oniru beach is a privately owned beach located at Eti-Osa Local government Area. The oniru beach is good for both swimming and picnics, however, it is exceptionally packed and uproarious on occasion. Horseback riding is accessible for an expense. The Oniru Beach is a 24 hours exclusive beach in Lagos Nigeria. The beach is owned by the Oniru Royal family. The Oniru Beach has a grill joint, suya (kind of meat) and fish spot, and a bar for assortments of drink.

The Oniru private beach has over the years being the favourites of many beach lovers in Nigeria. Some always categorize the beach as the a place for the rich because it is privately owned. The beach in most cases doesn't give room to externals without proper check and gate fee payment.

It is also a regular Sunday spot for families and friends, as some people go as far as celebrating notable events around the beach.

The Oniru beach is populated during the festive period because it plays host to so many events and parties and the New Year. It also serves as a tourist attraction to guest in the country.

The beach is located in the Eti-Osa area of Lagos State. The fact that Lagos is home to some many beaches is quite different from the existence of the Oniru beach. The beach is widely acceptable for couples on a date and also offers so much more like the horse riding, also there is always a hut for you to rent for you and your friends. it helps in creating a classy and unique style for the beach

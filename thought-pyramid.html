Millennium Park

Thought Pyramid Art Centre is an independent, non-profit, modern and contemporary art space in Abuja and Lagos, Nigeria. Its mission is to collect, conserve, and study modern and contemporary art of Africa, and the African Diaspora. We will exhibit the art of our times from a variety of perspectives while situating them with the context of a global art movement. We are driven to to the diversity of African art practice, we will promote understanding, appreciation, and enjoyment of art and its values in an accessible, contemporary and unpretentious environment..

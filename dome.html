DOME

The Dome is Nigeria’s iconic prime entertainment and hospitality center located in the heart of the Federal Capital City. It is a Proudly Nigerian private initiative created as an indigenous brand to offer you options for dining, relaxation, fitness, business meetings and entertainment.

Situated in Abuja’s Diplomatic Zone in Central Area; The Dome, which first began operations in 2005, is now reloaded. and repackaged to become a world class luxury destination for recreation, leisure and conferencing. You’ll always fall in love with The Dome.

We invite you to come, relax participate in our offering of luxury, pleasure, fun and wholesome entertainment in the city of Abuja. #DestinationDome
